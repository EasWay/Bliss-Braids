# Product Overview

Bliss Braids is a modern braiding salon booking website for the Ghanaian market. The system uses a "Request-First" workflow where customers customize their braiding style (service, size, length) and submit booking requests without online payment.

## Core Flow

1. Customer browses portfolio and services
2. Customer builds their style through a multi-step wizard
3. System calculates price dynamically based on selections
4. Customer submits booking request with contact details
5. Owner receives email notification with all booking details
6. Owner contacts customer via WhatsApp to arrange 50 GHS deposit via Mobile Money
7. Exact studio location shared after deposit confirmation

- Privacy-focused location sharing (radius circle, not exact pin)

## Target Market

Ghanaian customers seeking professional braiding services in the Spintex/East Legon area of Accra. Mobile-first design optimized for local internet speeds and payment preferences.
